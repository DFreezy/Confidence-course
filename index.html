Become confident in 30 days

This course was created to help shy guys step out of their comfort zones, and become confident

Day 1

Approach one female stranger

- Remember to count down from 5. This will start the course by forcing you to take chances

Day 2

Organize your computer

- Organizing your computer subconciously organizes your mind

Day 3

Take a cold bath

- Cold baths helps you tackle the day ahead more effectively

Day 4

Prepare keywords to say per day

- This will force you to speak more even if your naturally silent

Day 5

Do 5 pushups in a crowded area

- This will make you immune to judgement

Day 6

Approach a girl with a cheesy pickup line

- This will help you to not take yourself so seriously

Day 7

Hold a female stranger's hand

- This will fill you with a sense of freedom and help destroy any social anxiety

Day 8

Do an evil phone call, and laugh in a crowded area

- This will set you apart from the rest, and fill you with confidence to stand out

Day 9

Have no caffeine, alcohol or sugar

- This will make you feel healthier

Day 10

Drink at least 2 Liters of water

This will make you feel more hydrated, and fill you with more energy
